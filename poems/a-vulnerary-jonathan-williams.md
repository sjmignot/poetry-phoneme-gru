# A Vulnerary ﻿
## Jonathan Williams
for Robert Duncan ﻿
one comes to language from afar, the ear
fears for its sound-barriers—

but one “comes”; the language “comes” for
 _The Beckoning Fair One_

over the water makes a
solid, six-sided music…

are very far away— _“form_
